Beaches

Dubai is a beach-lover’s paradise; sun, sand and crystal-blue waters are a year-round treat. Dubai’s beaches offer plenty of water adventures & sports to dive into, tempting dining spots line up the boardwalks, and spectacular views of the Dubai skyline and horizon.

Also Try: Best Private Beaches in Dubai (Do NOT Miss Out!)

1. Jumeirah Beach Residence Beach – Most Popular Beach in Dubai

JBR Beach is easily one of the most popular beaches in Dubai for tourists & residents alike. It has all the amenities you could want and is next to one of Dubai’s best promenades: The Walk at JBR. This beach’s popularity can lead to crowds occasionally; this is particularly true when an event is being held. Also Try: Best Attractions in Jumeirah (STUNNING!)

Bathrooms & Showers: Available
Cost: Free
Location: Jumeirah Beach Residence, Dubai, United Arab Emirates
Dubaing Score™: 5/5

2. Burj Beach – Best Beach for Dubai Pictures

If you enjoy photography, this beach is the perfect location to get jaw-dropping pictures of Dubai’s icon and this beach’s namesake: Burj al Arab. The beach includes most sports facilities, including a running track, and dining spots are within convenient reach. It’s also next to one of the Dubai’s most beautiful souks (Madinat Jumeirah). Also Try: Best Ways to get Sky Views of Dubai (You’ll LOVE it!)

Bathrooms & Showers: Available
Cost: Free
Location: Jumeirah, Dubai, United Arab Emirates
Dubaing Score™: 5/5

3. Jumeirah Open Beach – Best Dawn/Dusk Beach in Dubai

Another wildly popular beach in Jumeirah that is close to Burj al Arab. The beach is somewhat small but it has all the beachness you could want: calm waters, sand, & sun. It offers great views of the city skyline & Burj al Arab. It’s also a short distance from the Palm Strip Mall. Also Try: Essential Things to See & Do in Dubai (Some are FREE!)

Bathrooms & Showers: Available
Cost: Free
Location: Jumeirah, Dubai, United Arab Emirates
Dubaing Score™: 5/5

4. Mamzar Beach – Best Family-Time Beach in Dubai

One of the largest beaches in Dubai, Mamzar Beach possibly has all the things you could want in a beach-side day. It’s adjacent to a massive park, it gives you access to barbeque pits, you can rent chalets & umbrellas, and life guards are on duty daily. It even has 2 large swimming pools nearby in case the open waters aren’t impressing you. Cafés and dining options are within easy reach. Also Try: Ranked Luxury Spas in Dubai (Surprisingly Cheap!)

Bathrooms & Showers: Available
Cost: AED 40 per car; AED 5 if entry by foot
Location: Mamzar, Dubai, United Arab Emirates
Dubaing Score™: 4.5/5

5. Kite Beach – Best Beach in Dubai for Water Sports

Kite Beach retains a heavy popularity among residents and has only recently developed attraction from tourists. It’s also close to Burj al Arab but this beach lacks some amenities unlike other beaches on this list. It’s prime popularity is driven by its width allowing plenty of water sports to be done with ease—even when a crowd builds up. Be sure to get your own food and water here. Also Try: Ranking Best Water Sports in Dubai (BEST fun!)

Bathrooms & Showers: Available
Cost: Free
Location: Jumeirah, Dubai, United Arab Emirates
Dubaing Score™: 4/5

6. Jumeirah Beach Park – Best Full-Day Beach in Dubai

This beach has been awarded the blue flag beach status and that has spurred its popularity. It blends sandy shores, grassy fields with palm trees (at a distance), and promenade-style dining options. It’s a full-day destination as you can enjoy the sand & water, sports in the grass, & some delicious food. Mondays here are termed “Ladies Day” and that limits entry to only women.

Bathrooms & Showers: Available
Cost: AED 5
Location: Jumeirah, Dubai, United Arab Emirates
Dubaing Score™: 4/5

7. 4x4 Beach – Best Car Beach in Dubai

This beach is highly popular among bay spectators who enjoy having a seat in the car and just meditating off with blank stares into the beautiful sand, water, and bay. It has a massive expanse of sand and is rarely crowded; you’ll have a lot of beach space all to yourself. If you prefer staying away from crowds, this is a spot for you. Be sure to bring your own water & food. Also Try: 5 World-Famous Dubai Car Exhibits (New 2017)

Bathrooms & Showers: N/A
Cost: Free
Location: Jumeirah, Dubai, United Arab Emirates
Dubaing Score™: 3.5/5

8. Jebel Ali Beach – Largest Free Beach in Dubai

This is a massive stretch of sandy shoes beside a resort–have no worries, the resort does not charge you for using this beach. This beach offers no crowds, large expanse of sand, and clear waters. You can even camp here but that needs a permit from the Dubai Municipality; that can be a bit time consuming.

Bathrooms & Showers: N/A
Cost: Free
Location: Jebel Ali, Dubai, United Arab Emirates
Dubaing Score™: 3.5/5

9. Black Palace Beach – Best Quiet Beach in Dubai (Tie)

This beach derives its name from its close proximity to Dubai’s royal fortress. Black Palace Beach offers stunning views of the Palm Jumeirah and allows you to relax in calm waters in the presence of very few other visitors. Also Try: Best Palaces & Forts to Visit in Dubai (Almost GONE 2017)

Bathrooms & Showers: N/A
Cost: Free
Location: Jumeirah, Dubai, United Arab Emirates
Dubaing Score™: 3.5/5

10. Hidden Beach – Best Quiet Beach in Dubai (Tie)

As the name hints, this is another beach away from the crowds. It’s located on the left side of Sheraton’s JBR resort. It’s the perfect place to take in the splendid views of the Jumeirah Lake Towers while enjoying the brilliance of the horizon meeting the water, as you lay in cozy, soft sand.

Bathrooms & Showers: N/A
Cost: Free
Location: Jumeirah, Dubai, United Arab Emirates
Dubaing Score™: 3.5/5
