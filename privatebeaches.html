Top 25 Private Beaches (Beach Clubs) in Dubai

Beach clubs are essentially private beach grounds with a lot of added splendor, but that splendor comes with a price tag. As you’d expect, Dubai hosts some of the most opulent and stunning beach clubs in the world that offer luxury & delight throughout the day and night.

1. Royal Island Beach – Best Private Beach (Beach Club) in Dubai

This is the first island open to the public on Dubai’s “The World” development. You are provided private chalets, a ferry to the island, private customer service, a restaurant, a swimming pool, and 2 beaches. Also, you’re on a man-made island’s beach! Additionally, due to limited entry spots, you would have quite a lot of space to yourself. Also Try: Essential Things to See &amp; Do in Dubai (Do NOT Miss Out!)

Cost: AED 300; 50% off for under 10s; Free for under 3s
Location: The World Development, Dubai, United Arab Emirates
Contact: (04) 447-2240
Dubaing Score™: 5/5

2. Nikki Beach – Best Guest-Exclusive Beach in Dubai

Nikki Beach is one of the newer beach clubs to hit Dubai and they’ve got their own island location: Pearl Jumeirah Island. Given that it’s on its own island, Nikki Beach has one of the most impressive and exclusive beaches in Dubai. Also Try: Most Popular Water Sports in Dubai (Some are SCARY!)
