1. Dubai Gravity Zone – Most Thrilling Height Attraction in Dubai

Here’s one for the true thrill seekers. Gravity Zone is Dubai’s prime bungee jumping offering. Don’t expect cliff-side views though. This bungee jump is based off a crane in an inner-city location. So pace up for an electrifying jump towards the ground at about 100 km/h! This would be a great opportunity to pleasantly take in some amazing views but the adrenaline rush might be an obstacle that. For those daring enough, the location offers a backwards jump too, and the romantics can do a tandem jump!

Gate Price: Starting at AED 290
Timing: 11 AM – 5.30 PM Tuesday, Friday, & Saturday; Closed all other days
Location: Power Play Football Center, Dubai, United Arab Emirates
Contact: http://www.gravityzone.co/location
Website: http://www.gravityzone.co/
Time Dedication: Less than 1 hour

Dubaing Score™: 5/5

2. Dubai Autodrome – Best Car Attration in Dubai

The Autodrome offers an experience that’s hard to replicate: driving an F1 race car! This one’s for you if you enjoy speed—extreme speed. Truth be told, you have the option to race with a Lotus, an Audi R8 V10, and a McLaren, but those units cost more than an F1 drive, which starts at AED 875. Though, the true uniqueness of this experience lies in racing in an F1 car rather than a standard sports car, and this isn’t just for petrol heads; everyone can enjoy the thrill of racing in a Formula 1 car and live up those childhood dreams.

The Autodrome also features modified go-kart races in case you aren’t up for a F1 experience or are on a tighter budget. Additionally, if you’re more of a spectator, check the calendar dates and you might get lucky enough to see a motorsport race.

Gate Price: F1 experience starting at AED 875; go-karts vary between AED 100 - 130
Timing: Daily 9 AM – 9 PM
Location: Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Road, Dubai, United Arab Emirates
Contact: (971) 04 367-8700
Website: www.dubaiautodrome.com/
Time Dedication: 2 to 4 hours
Dubaing Score™: 5/5

5. Hub Zero Dubai – Best Gamer Attraction in Dubai

Hub Zero is a dream experience for gamers. The location’s highlight is immersive game simulation in which you team up with other players and are then “transported” to an action-packed battlefield where you defeat your enemy to reign in glorious victory. Hub Zero’s other attractions feature premium VR and 4D gaming experiences. Once you’ve exhausted your quote of unique games, you can head to the lounge to grab a bite and play pool with friends or family. While you’re here, be sure to enjoy a stroll in Dubai City Walk; one of the liveliest streets you’ll ever see!

Gate Price: AED 100 for the highlight
Timing: Daily 2 PM – 10 PM; Thursday & Friday 2 PM - Midnight
Location: City Walk, Dubai, United Arab Emirates
Contact: 800 637-227
Website: www.hubzerodubai.com/

6. Mushrif Park Dubai – Most Challenging Nature Adventure in Dubai

Dubai is a growing concrete jungle and that’s why it’s comforting to know that Mushrif Park holds the closest things to a Dubai forest; it primarily Dubai’s native ghaf trees. Mushrif hosts 80 activities divided into 6 different courses. Prepare for some outdoor adventure and fun with log balancing, rope walks, zip lines, and more! Discovery and Rangers circuits are for children, Explorador is great for families to venture on an adventure together, Aventura serves young adults, and Thriller & Xtreme circuits are for the ones seeking a daring challenge. Beside challenging adventures, this park is a natural wonder and offers an opportunity to delve into nature’s beauty in Dubai.

Gate Price: AED 150 for Adults; AED 125 for children
Timing: Daily 9.30 AM to 6 PM
Location: Mirdif, Dubai, United Arab Emirates
Contact: (971) 056 887-1687
Website: http://www.wadiadventure.ae/
Time Dedication: An hour to stroll through the park; the attractions will take up to 2 hours
Dubaing Score™: 4/5

7. BONUS: Via Ferrata in Ras al Khaimah – Best Hiking Attraction in UAE

While not in Dubai, this is an enjoyable and challenging adventure set in UAE’s Hajar Mountains. There are various courses here that vary by difficulty so just about everyone is welcome here. The longest course is possibly the most enjoyable as it features 3 zip lines which can be 300m long. While this can be, on average, a 90-minute trip from Dubai, it’s worth a visit as you get to both see the Hajar Mountains and enjoy an challenging adventure park. Keep in mind that this adventure park has only seasonal openings (generally in October).

Gate Price: AED 400
Timing: Varies; seasonal opening (generally in October)
Location: Hajar Mountains; Ras al Khaimah, United Arab Emirates
Contact: N/A
Website: https://jebeljais.ae/via-ferrata/
Time Dedication: 2 – 4 hours; depends on course taken
Dubaing Score™: 4/5
