The 5 Best Theme Parks in Dubai

An extensive amount of theme parks have sprouted in Dubai and, impressively, each can guarantee a unique experience. Here’s the most authoritative ranking of Dubai’s best theme parks.

IMG Worlds of Adventure – Best Theme Park in Dubai (Tie)

This park takes the cake merely by being colossal despite being indoors! In fact, it’s the world’s largest indoor theme park. With 4 “epic” subsections, rest assured that park visitors will have plenty of fun; the park has 17 themed rides. The more renowned sections feature attractions and restaurants/shops based on Cartoon Network and Marvel characters.
Details: You’ll enjoy thundering roller coasters and a live stage show. The third “epic” section, named Lost Valley-Dinosaur Adventure, is seeded with an everything-dinosaur theme for rides and shops. The fourth section features just one “ride,” which is a haunted hotel; the rest of the fourth section (IMG Boulevard) is essentially a lobby for the rest of the park, but it is studded with shops. Dubai Parks and Resorts (Collection of 3 Parks)

This is a collection of multiple theme parks in one location under the umbrella of one massive company. The location is outdoors and is reminiscent of colossal theme parks like Disney World and Universal Studios.

However, it combines the themes of multiple Hollywood and Bollywood studios to offer one of the most exciting mixture of rides, cinematic experiences, and décor in the world. It features MOTIONGATE Dubai, Bollywod Parks Dubai, and Legoland Dubai. Six Flags Dubai is planned to launch in 2019. Each of the parks is stuffed with shops and restaurants.

The location also includes a promenade (lively walking area) called Riverland Dubai. Ranking: Top 5 Promenades in Dubai (FREE!)

MOTIONGATE Dubai – Best Theme Park in Dubai (Tie)

This park features 26 attractions and utilizes many themes from various Hollywood studios. It incorporates Underworld, Hotel Transylvania, Ghostbusters, and many other movies produced by Columbia Pictures into roller coasters, cinematic experiences, and rides perfect for all ages. The park hosts Smurfs Village, a quaint fictitious town that will bring joy to both children and fans of the Smurfs movie. There’s more: the park also partnered with DreamWorks Studio to create rides based on some of the best Hollywood movies like Madagascar, Shrek, How to Train Your Dragon, and Kung Fu Panda. To add to the exhilaration, Lionsgate partnered in to bring rides themes on Hunger Games and Step up!

This one park features such fresh variety of rides and magical décor that it can be argued that MOTIONGATE Dubai is a competitor for one of the best theme parks in the world. Dubaing Score™: 5/5

Bollywood Parks – Best Bollywood Themed Attraction in Dubai

The first of its kind! This park is entirely cored upon Bollywood movies and the traditional Indian cinematic experience. It incorporates cutting edge technology to create a Bollywood rival of Hollywood studio theme parks. Themes and characters from Krrish, Dabangg, Sholay, Lagaan, and more prominent Bollywood movies are developed into thrilling rides, cinematic experiences, and live performances. The park’s crown jewel, the Rajmahal Theatre, brings a taste of Broadway-style performances to Dubai, but with a twist: it features unique Bollywood musicals that will test the bounds of your imagination and bring you into colorful and joyous world of splendor. LEGOLAND Dubai – Best Children’s Theme Park in Dubai

The contents of this park are obviously clear from its name: it features everything Lego! The park has only one roller coaster but still features plenty of attractions. It gives a tour of how legos are made, includes a mini-Dubai made entirely out of Legos, many kid-friendly rides, and Lego playrooms. Dubaing Score™: 4/5

Extension of LEGOLAND Dubai: LEGOLAND Water Park

We’ve covered some intense water rides; so this one’s for the kids! LEGOLAND as a whole is catered primarily for family fun, especially families with kids aged 2 – 12. That’s why this parks excels at catering to that age group. But don’t worry, this water park is still exceptionally fun.

Highlights: Unique concepts like customizing your own raft out of Legos and then taking it for a dive! The park has plenty of water slides that offer exhilarating dives and swings that’d give all ages some fun. They are not as wild as the slides at Aquaventure and Wild Wadi, but there’s plenty of splashing and wet fun! Ranking: Best FREE Attractions in Dubai (Yes, FREE!)Dubaing Score™: 3/5

BONUS: Ferrari World Abu Dhabi – Best Roller Coaster Park in UAE

Ferrari World is located in Abu Dhabi but it deserves a mention on this list as it’s an award-winning amusement park. And if you’re visiting Dubai, Abu Dhabi has a thing or two you might want to see, and it’s not too far off. The prime highlights here are the world’s fastest roller coaster and the opportunity to drive a real Ferrari!

The park also hosts 4 other roller coasters, 4 shows, and about a dozen child-friendly rides. This is a must-visit for car enthusiasts and Ferrari fans. Ranking: Best Things to Do in Abu Dhabi (Surprised?)
