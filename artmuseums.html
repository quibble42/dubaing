Dubai’s Best Art Museums

Art is beautiful, inspiring, and powerful. It moves our hearts, minds, and even inspires revolutions; therefore a thorough detail of where in Dubai to appreciate mankind’s most prominent form of expression is a must. Dubai and the rest of the United Arab Emirates presently possess a small inventory of art museums, but breathtaking museums like the Louvre and Guggenheim are on the way!

Here’s the best guide to discover which art museums currently exist in the UAE and what is on the way!

1. Dubai Moving Images Museum – Best Photography Museum in Dubai

A museum dedicated to photography and optics. It features pieces dating back to the twelfth century! The museum is quite interactive as it allows you to not only touch, but also use replicas, and sometimes even originals, of archaic photo tools. It’s established through the private collection of a businessman who hopes to spread his joy for photographic arts. Also Try: Essential Things to See & Do in Dubai (Do NOT Miss Out!)

Keep in mind that this museum is very small.

Cost: AED 50 for adults, AED 25 for children or students with valid ID
Timings: daily from 11am to 6pm; except Friday
Contact: (971) 04 421-6679
Dubaing Score™: 4.5/5

2. BONUS: Sharjah Art Museum – Best Art Museum in UAE

The museum offers large galleries and exhibitions giving visitors an opportunity to appreciate extremely large collections of art. The museum hosts rotating seasonal exhibitions and regular programs and workshops that interact with visitors. It stands 3 stories high and features over 500 unique pieces.
