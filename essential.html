11 Essential Things to Do in Dubai

With so much to do in Dubai, it’s likely you won’t have enough time to try everything if you are a tourist–residents can enjoy everything as per their liking. Most things in Dubai are enjoyable but some are just an essential experience.

1. Explore Dubai with a Desert Safari – Essential Things to Do in Dubai

The safari is a trip of a life-time: extremely enjoyable yet extremely affordable. Get ready for a fast and thrilling experience through the ups and downs of the desert dunes!

These will follow up with optional 4x4 or BMX rides through the desert, camel rides, and a night full of events ranging from colorful and vibrant traditional Arab dances to jaw-dropping belly dances. Of course, there’ll also be delicious BBQ meals for the guests; vegetarian diets are also supported.

Most safari operators will request you be picked up from a specific rendezvous, and a few will offer VIP packages which will involve a hotel pick-up. The safari operators work along with a desert camp operator to build an amazing experience. Dubai desert safaris take up 8 hours with all the amazing things they include. Avoid branding frills and focus on what extra features your money buys. Also Try: Ranking–Best Adrenaline Adventures in Dubai (Too much fun!)

Average Price: AED 125
Time Dedication: 6–8 hours
Dubaing Score™: 5/5

2. Relax in a Traditional Dubai Dhow – Essential Things to Do in Dubai

The dhows are large traditional Arab boats: subtly lit, wooden, and hosting many activities. Unlike the thrills of a safari, these offer a more calm and relaxing environment as you drift through waters flowing through Dubai, capturing the best skyline in the world. Dhow trips are like a mini cruise and will last for a few hours, allowing you to enjoy a scrumptious meal, accommodating all sorts of diets. Dhow trips can take between 2 to 4 hours. Average Price: AED 150
Time Dedication: 2–4 hours

3. See Dubai’s Iconic Burj Al Arab – Essential Things to Do in Dubai

Dubai is studded with iconic creations but one stands out as it has been crowned the world’s only 7-star hotel. A thing every resident & tourist seeks to do in Dubai: Visit Burj Al Arab. You can get great photos with the Burj by either visiting it or by standing on the edge of the Madinat Jumeirah’s entrance bridge. Getting in can be expensive as you are required to book something to do inside the hotel, and everything inside runs a high price tag.

To get in, most people opt for a meal or tea. The cheapest price for even the tea is AED 300, however, if you call and reserve a haircut, you’d not only get hair styled from the world’s only 7-star hotel, but will also get entry inside the Burj al Arab for AED 200—the cheapest possible cost of entry.

When you arrive at the gate of the Burj al Arab’s entrance, a security member will ask to see your reservation; be prepared with an e-mail, text message, or print-out of your reservation. Also Try: Best Things to Do in Jumeirah (FREE!)

Minimum Price: AED 200 (Haircut)/AED 300 (Tea)
Time Dedication: 1—3 hours
Dubaing Score™: 5/5

5. Air Balloon Rides – Essential Things to Do in Dubai

Dubai’s air balloon rides allow you to get the magical sights of the desert during dusk and dawn, as the sun paints the sky a reddish orange, and an opportunity to see the Dubai city from the heights and lows of your desires of all the locations. This is one of the most memorable things to do in Dubai.

6. Dubai Helicopter Rides – Essential Things to Do in Dubai

Dubai has many companies competing to offer the most exciting experience with helicopters; if sky views are your joy, this is something you must do. The experience, though, is usually short and as the trip lasts no more than 30 minutes. But those 30 minutes will be nothing less than a magical memory.

The unarguable champion for the experience of a lifetime in Dubai, while offering unimaginable views of the city: Sky Diving. Sky diving views in Dubai are so brilliant that they’ve made their way into popular song videos. This honor is easily well-earned & is a destination thing-to-do in Dubai.

8. Visit the Burj al Khalifa – Essential Things to Do in Dubai

Dubai’s newest global icon is also its greatest attraction: The Burj Khalifa. Towering over the city with the title of “World’s Tallest Building,” the Burj is something you must visit when in Dubai. The Dubai Mall, Dubai Fountain, and the bustling Sheikh Mohammed Boulevard are right next to it.

Average Price: FREE
Time Dedication: 1–2 Hours
Dubaing Score™: 5/5

9. Spectate the Dubai Fountain’s Dance – Essential Things to Do in Dubai

The Dubai Fountain is a world dancing champion and you get to see its marvelous moves every 30 minutes. Situated right between Dubai Mall & Burj Khalifa, the mall’s surrounding is all too spectacular. This is also one of the free things to do in Dubai!

Average Price: FREE
Time Dedication: 30 Minutes–1 Hour
Dubaing Score™: 5/5

10. Ski in the Summer – Essential Things to Do in Dubai

It’s hard to imagine that today you can ski in the middle of a desert during the summer heat. Having a ski trip at Mall of the Emirates’ Ski Dubai is one of the ultimate things to do in Dubai. Don’t fret if you cannot ski: there is an exciting snow park there too; it’s packed with many snow-covered activities.

Average Price: AED 225
Time Dedication: 2–4 Hours
