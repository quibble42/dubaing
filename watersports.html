28 Most Popular Water Sports & Activities in Dubai

Dubai is studded with sandy beaches, and the skies remain bright and sunny year-round! The best part is that you can do a lot more than just tan and build sand castles here. Dubai’s beaches support plenty of water thrills, and some activities are so popular that the beaches have been named after the activity (like Kitesurfing Beach). Prepare for an electrifying and extremely fun time with Dubai’s water sports and activities; enjoy this list! Guides and coaches are always available for these activities. This list is divided; the first portion features the most common attractions and then we list the unique ones.

Renowned Water Sports & Activities in Dubai

1. Special Featuring: Stand-up Paddleboards in Dubai – Most Popular Tourist Water Sport

This is Dubai’s world-renowned water destination. The names says it all! You stand up on a paddleboard and paddle to your destination. It’s quite an exercise and it offers some great views of Dubai as you paddle off the beaches or around the Burj al Arab. Guides and coaches are always available. Also Try: Top 10 Free Beaches in Dubai (Yes, FREE!)

Wet Rating: 3/5
Wild Rating: 1/5
Average Price: AED 60 for 30 minutes
Dubaing Score™: 3.5/5

2. Snorkeling – Most Globally Popular Underwater Attraction (Tie)

Dubai is studded with spots that support snorkeling into clear-blue waters. You can enjoy swimming deep in the water with all sorts of fish across the city. The most impressive experience, though, is at Atlantis the Palm. You get to take a dive into a Ambassador Lagoon and swim with 65,000 sea creatures, including a hammerhead shark—that’s a true Dubai experience! It’s all completely safe. Guides and coaches are always available. Also Try: Best Water Parks in Dubai (6 is Shocking!)

Wet Rating: 10/5
Wild Rating: 3/5 (5 if you go for the shark)
Average Price: AED 255 at Atlantis, Dubai
Dubaing Score™: 5/5

3. Diving – Most Globally Popular Underwater Attraction (Tie)

Of course, diving was going to be a part of this guide. Dubai has many amazing opportunities for diving and you can get up close with eels, squids, octopuses, puffer fish, and even a (completely safe) whale shark! There are great opportunities to explore waters in Musandam and Fujairah. Guides and coaches are always available.

Wet Rating: 5/5
Wild Rating: 5/5
Average Price: AED 150 per dive (additional costs for gear)
Dubaing Score™: 5/5

4. Parasailing Dubai – Easiest Water Thrill in Dubai

Get ready to fly! This is one of the most enjoyable things to do in Dubai and it brings on some spectacular views. You are connected to a sail which is attached to a speedboat. As the boat moves, you fly up in the air. This is a mix of relaxation and thrill; the boat speeds and you sail through the air, but quickly you are put at ease and begin to enjoy the amazing views of Dubai skyline. Guides and coaches are always available. Also Try: Dubai’s World Famous Car Exhibits (FREE!)

Wet Rating: 1/5
Wild Rating: 1/5
Average Price: AED 250 per ride
Dubaing Score™: 5/5

5. Flyboarding Dubai – Best Water Thrill in Dubai

Fly! Boarding! Dubai has welcomed this amazing phenomenon with open arms as this is an experience available at beaches across the emirate. Suit up into a water-jet propelled machine. Your feet are strapped to a board and your wrist are locked into the controls. After that you are in command of your flight! It’s easy to see why flyboarding is such a sensation in Dubai. Based on how much water you press out and how you direct yourself, you can fly in any direction at the speed you want.

Guides and coaches are always available.

Wet Rating: 5/5
Wild Rating: 10/5
Average Price: AED 300 per 30 minutes
Dubaing Score™: 5/5

Rising Water Sports & Activities

6. Kitesurfing Dubai – Most Popular Beach Sport in Dubai

A globally popular spot, but spectacularly fun in Dubai! Kitesurfing involves you being settled on a surf board and massive kite is attached to you. The kite flows around with the wind and drives you at rapid motion. Things quickly get fast and furious with this sport! Guides and coaches are always available.

Wet Rating: 5/5
Wild Rating: 5/5
Average Price: AED 100 per 45 mins
Dubaing Score™: 5/5

7. Dragon Boats in Dubai – Best Foreign Water Sport in Dubai

Dubai is a multicultural destination. The tradition of dragon boats is celebrated here each April in Dubai Festival City. A dragon boat is a large vessel designed to resemble a dragon and is propelled by a large team of paddlers; the competitiveness is intense and enjoyable. To participate, you’ll need to be part of a team, but everyone is welcome to spectate. If you’re travelling to Dubai in April, do give this a shot; it could be an awesome Dubai memory!

Wet Rating: 1/5
Wild Rating: 1/5 (5 if you compete)
Average Price: Free to Spectate
Dubaing Score™: 5/5

8. Shark Safari Dubai – Most Exciting Underwater Adventure in Dubai

You don’t need to be a good diver for this; in fact you don’t even need to know how to swim—you walk through this underwater safari! Dubai’s Atlantis at the Palm offers you an amazing opportunity to walk at the bottom of massive water tank that contains sharks, rays, and thousands of colorful fish. It’s all extremely safe and you’re given a special helmet to wear as you stroll at the bottom of the tank. Guides and coaches are always available.

Wet Rating: 10/5
Wild Rating: 1/5
Average Price: AED 275 for entry
Dubaing Score™: 5/5

9. Jet Skiing Dubai – Globally Popular Water Sport

This is perhaps the most enjoyable water sport and it is also the easiest! Jet skis are the iconic symbol for water-filled, sunny, and fun vacation. Dubai is studded with spots that offer jet ski rentals so you can speed through the water and perhaps even race with your friends and/or family. Guides and coaches are always available. Also Try: Top Beach Clubs in Dubai

Wet Rating: 3/5
Wild Rating: 5/5
Average Price: AED 300 for 30 minutes
Dubaing Score™: 4.5/5

10. Kneeboarding Dubai – Rising Water Sport in Dubai

A milder version of surfing, kneeboarding has gained popularity in recent years. The best part is that you don’t need to acquire the skills of balancing on a board, you can be seated upon your knees; hence the name kneeboarding. Speed through water on hydro-dynamic boards designed to give you an awesome time. Guides and coaches are always available.

Wet Rating: 5/5
Wild Rating: 4/5
Average Price: AED 150 per 15 mins
Dubaing Score™: 4/5

11. Windsurfing Dubai – Rising Water Thrill Sport in Dubai

The wind, a sailboard, and you—it’s that simple! Jump on to a sailboard and tackle the wind and water on a thrilling experience as you and your sailboard challenge nature in Dubai. These aren’t motorized so winds command your pace; if you are new to this, you can stay closer to the shore and face light winds and a mild pace. Guides and coaches are always available.

Wet Rating: 5/5
Wild Rating: 5/5
Average Price: AED 150 per 30 mins
Dubaing Score™: 4/5

12. Wakeboarding Dubai – Rising, Challenging Water Sport in Dubai

This is a challenging water sport in Dubai. Your feet are strapped to a board and you hold on to a handle that is tied to a speedboat at a distance. The speedboat takes off and so do you! Get ready for a fast, wild, and fun ride as you spear through the water on your wakeboard. This is a global sensation that is only more fun at Dubai’s sandy and sunny beaches. Be ready for a lot of plunges into the water. Guides and coaches are always available.

Wet Rating: 5/5
Wild Rating: 5/5
Average Price: AED 150 per 15 mins
Dubaing Score™: 4/5

13. Wakesurfing Dubai – Popular Resort Water Sport in Dubai

This is a unique mix of surfing and wakeboarding in Dubai. You stand on a surfboard (your feet are not tied down) and you hold on to a handle attached to speedboat. The speedboat races on and you get a thrilling ride as you pierce through the water, twirling left and right as you desire. This truly lets you command the thrill and freedom of your movement. Guides and coaches are always available.

Wet Rating: 5/5
Wild Rating: 4/5
Average Price: AED 140 for 15 mins
Dubaing Score™: 4/5

14. Waterskiing Dubai – Globally Popular Water Sport

Waterskiing is one of the world’s most popular water leisure; Dubai’s tourism is no exception to this popularity. Your feet on a ski board and your hands on a handle attached to a speedboat. This thrill-ride will give you some awesome Dubai memories. Guides and coaches are always available.

This is an intensive exercise and extremely fun. Dubai offers many opportunities to kayak on its shores. The most impressive time is kayaking around he curves of the Palm Jumeirah. You can kayak away from the shore and settle for a beautiful dawn view of the Dubai skyline with friends, family, or that one special person. Guides and coaches are always available.

17. Donut Ride – Most Thrilling Local Water Attraction (Tie)

Another novel water activity in Dubai! These resemble inflatable beds (or couches) with grips and can accompany up to 4 people at a time. Despite the name, most of them do not have a hole in the center. The fun begins when a speedboat propels the attached inflatables, giving you a fast-paced blast through the water dotted with sharp turns. Guides and coaches are always available.
