Dubai Destination Spas

Destination spas are premier, refined, and unique to the point they are worthy of being a landmark destination of Dubai. Dubai’s destination spas are characterized by extreme luxury with a sense of modernity to the design and amenities. Your soothing massages, facials, pedicures, scrubs, and so much more will take place besides beautiful lagoon-style pools, lush gardens, or private chambers brilliantly designed to boost your relaxation.

Some offer you a soothing experience as you enjoy a breathtaking view of a sunset across Dubai’s skyline or a sunrise over the Arabian sea. At these Dubai spas, you will not see any flick of plain tiles or walls, no poolside will go unadorned by fine marble or designed plating, and no pillar will hang without a resemblance to the royal premise.

Essentially, each feature will be designed to specifically match a soothing mood and environment that will allow you to feel pampered in a luxury like never before; Destination spas have a 5/5 Dubaing Score™.

Destination Dubai Hammams

Hammams are traditional spas that feature décor resembling chambers of royalty. And the treatment you get is not short of “royal treatment.” Dubai Hammams are designed with opulence, gold, fine marble, soothing art, and luxuriously styled pools, saunas, and Jacuzzis; you’ll have a real oasis experience.

Hammams sooth your body and invigorate a glow in your skin. They use a gentle heat to release your body’s toxins as a soothing scrub washes away dead skin. You will step out of a Hammam feeling soothed, relaxed, and happy.

Dubai Ayurvedic Spas

An Ayurvedic spa builds off the traditional healing and relaxation practices of South Asia. Dubai has set the global standard of Ayuvedic spas. You will enter a moment of peace at Ayurvedic spas; the settings are calm, the air is wondrous (due to the burning sage), and the power of traditional Ayurveda will release your body’s tensions and allow you to feel gleeful.

The ingredients are organic, natural, and designed to help you relax as you get treatments including massages, reflexology, beauty cleanses, and much more—all designed to soothe, relax, and renew your body.
